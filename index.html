About NICA

Nigerian Infection Control Association(NICA) is a multidisciplinary voluntary organization committed to improving patient care and promoting occupational and public health. Our aim is to influence, support and improve the quality of healthcare delivery in Nigeria through the practice of infection control policies..

Expectations

NCIC 2016 Expectations

Experience

NCIC will present experiences from across the nation to help understand and influence appropriate infection prevention and control practices and highlight the different approaches needed to bring about effective change. The conference promises to be one to have significant impact on infection prevention and control in our nation.

Emerging Infections

There will also be a focus on new emerging infections which pose a public health threat to all nations. One should also look forward to:

  • Pre-conference workshop
  • Key note lecture
  • Plenary sessions
  • Oral Abstract presentations
  • Poster abstract rounds
  • Symposia/Round table session
  • General meeting
  • Welcome cocktail and other activities.

Research

Delegates will have an ample opportunity to share their researches and experiences through oral and poster presentations.

International Expatriates

It will also be a forum for educational experiences through expert sessions/symposia by international experts, with opportunity to participate in every session and time for networking.

Antimicrobial Stewardship Programs

There will be sharing of knowledge and exchange of experiences in the prevention of healthcare associated infections, the control of antimicrobial resistance and the implementation of antimicrobial stewardship programs.
